World Robot Olympiad

World Robot Olympiad (WRO)

WRO is an international robotics competition for students from around the world. This competition uses super cool Lego Mindstorms made by LEGO Education!

It started in 2004 in Singapore and now over 28,000 teams from 85 countries join in on the fun!

There are 3 categories:

-> Elementary (8-12 years old)

-> Junior (11-15 years old)

-> Senior (14-19 years old)

  • -> You will get amazing experiences and memories
  • -> You meet new friends
  • -> You practice teamwork
  • -> You learn, test, and get better at coding!
  • -> Show-off your creativity and problem-solving skills with robots
  • -> Discover ways to help make the world a better place!

(As you saw in the video!)

Find your members: 3 student make the team.

  • 1- Designer:incharge of designing the robot.
  • 2- Programmer: incharge of programming the robot.
  • 3- Observer:: incharge of making observation, taking notes, and find ways to improve.

Have your robot built and programmed, ready to win the competition

Tips and advices

  1. 1- Enjoy the process. Bond with other students who are interested in Robotics, there's much to learn from everybody.
  2. 2- Think outside the box. Sometimes the solution is through the simplest path.
  3. 3-Check the values of your variables first. Everything in your surroundings may affect the sensors.
  4. 4-Take extra motors, extra batteries, extra sensors and extra pieces, just in case.
  5. 5-Maximize the time through team work, everyone in the team should be engaged in testing, arranging pieces, providing feedback, noticing errors.
  6. 6-Take notes on every test you do: The mistakes and adjustments.
  7. 7-Teamwork: All the team members need to be at the table because some of them could notice something that the others can’t see.
  8. 8-Your computer and robot needs to be fully charged at the beginning of the day
